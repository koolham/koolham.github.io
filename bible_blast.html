How to Play

  • 1
    Read the challenge A scripture challenge appears at the top of the screen ‚Äî fill in the blank, identify the theme, name the testament, or recall the verse address.
  • 2
    Tap the correct tile A grid of word tiles drops onto the screen. Tap the tile that correctly answers the challenge before the timer runs out.
  • 3
    Avoid wrong answers Each incorrect tap costs a life. Lose all three hearts and the game is over.
  • 4
    Beat your high score Chain correct answers to rack up points. Your best score is saved locally so you can always try to top it.
