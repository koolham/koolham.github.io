Virtual Hamster

A cozy virtual pet game — care for adorable pixel-art hamsters

Gentle Pet Care Adorable Hamsters Cozy Pixel World Calm Gameplay Family Friendly Easy To Pick Up

A relaxing virtual pet experience where you care for hamsters in a pixel-art cage. Feed, water, exercise, and clean up after your fuzzy companions — but keep them alive, or it's game over. The free version includes one hamster with a 10-minute session limit; upgrade to the full version for unlimited play with two customizable hamsters and full personality selection.

🤖 Google Play — Coming Soon

How to Play

  • 1
    Create your hamsters Name, customize the color, and choose the personality for each hamster. (Full version only — free version has one random hamster and a 10-minute session limit)
  • 2
    Keep them alive Monitor hunger, thirst, energy, stress, sickness, and age. Tap the food bowl to feed or water bottle to hydrate when their stats drop.
  • 3
    Manage their wellbeing Hamsters exercise spontaneously — turn off lights to help them sleep faster, tap them to reduce stress, and double-tap poop to clean the cage and prevent sickness spread.
  • 4
    Earn points and survive Score points by caring for your hamsters. If all hamsters die, the game is over. Keep them happy and healthy as long as possible.

Gameplay Features

  • Free vs Full Version — Free includes one hamster with random properties and a 10-minute session limit; full version unlocks unlimited play with two hamsters and full customisation (name, color, personality)
  • Real-Time Pet Stats — Hunger, thirst, energy, stress, sickness, and age change continuously — requires active management
  • Interactive Gameplay — Tap to feed, tap to water, drag the food bowl to reposition it, double-tap poop to clean it up, turn lights on/off, and pet hamsters to reduce stress
  • Multiple Animations — Walking, running, sleeping, stressed, full, starving, and dead states with smooth 2-frame animation cycles
  • Pixel-Art Procedural Graphics — All visuals (cage, hamsters, objects, UI) are drawn procedurally — zero image files, resolution-independent
  • Persistent Game State — Hamster data and settings saved locally; resume your game anytime
  • In-App Purchase — One-time non-consumable purchase to unlock the full version with offline verification

Highlights

  • Cozy Gameplay — No time limits, no combat, no violence—just caring for cute virtual pets
  • Retro Pixel Aesthetic — Inspired by 80s arcade games, with charming pixel-art hamsters and cage
  • Landscape Orientation — Optimized for landscape play on all platforms (iOS, Android, macOS)
  • Offline Play — Plays offline after purchase verification; works without active internet connection
  • No External Assets — Fully self-contained rendering with procedural graphics—fast load, small download size
